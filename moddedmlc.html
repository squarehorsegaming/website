Getting Started

Welcome to the official page for our Modded Minecraft server!

As of now, the server is currently in a testing phase. Expect progress to be wiped at the end of testing.
The server is currently only open to our testers, and not the general public, sorry!

The modpack itself, and more information about it, can be found here.

Requirements:

  • Around 500MB's of hard drive/SSD space
  • At least 5GB's of RAM

    • How to install the pack:

    Download the Twitch launcher from here: https://app.twitch.tv/download
  • Once it's installed, click on the mods tab, then click Minecraft.
  • Press the "browse all modpacks" tab, and then search for for "SHG".
  • Click on the install button, and wait for it to download. This may take a while, so be patient.

    • How to allocate additional RAM, and additional Java arguments:

  • If you haven't already, you'll need to allocate more memory to Minecraft via the Twitch app.
  • To do this, click on the down arrow near the top right of the client, and click settings.
  • Navigate down the left tab to the Minecraft tab, and scroll down to the Java Settings section.
  • Set the slider to 5,120 MB+. Any less than this and you'll likely have massive loading times.
  • Scroll further down to the Advanced section, and copy and paste this into the "Additional Java Arguments" area.

    • How to play on the server:

  • Navigate to the Mods tab, click on Minecraft. Press the Play button on the pack labeled "SHG - Modded Mine Little Crafty".
  • Wait for the pack to start. Depending on your PC specs, this could take anywhere from 4-5 minutes, to 30+ minutes. Blame Forge. :^)
  • Once it's done loading, click on multiplayer, press "Add Server", and enter in the following ip in the Server Address box. The server is currently in a testing phase, and is not open to the general public, sorry!
  • Click on the server in the menu, and press Join server.
  • You're all done! Enjoy our server!

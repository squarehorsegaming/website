Frequently Asked Questions

Q: My build on the Modded Minecraft server got griefed, what do I do?

A: Report it to a staff member and we’ll get it taken care of for you as soon as we can. Otherwise, if you witness someone griefing another player’s project, you can also report that, or post on Discord in the #serverhelp channel.

Q: Is Griefing/Stealing permitted on your servers?

A: No, and any such actions can result in your account being banned. Refer to our full list of rules here: Square Horse Rules.

Q: A player is harassing me, but there's no staff around- what do I do?

A: You can ignore or block them for the time being, take screenshots of the given player’s offenses and either post it on Discord in #serverhelp or alert a staff member when they come online. Don’t take matters into your own hands- any rules you break in retaliation can still get you punished as well.

Q: I’ve been banned and I'd like to appeal, how do I do that?

A: Check the ban section of our forums here: Square Horse Bans & Appeals. You can also contact our staff via Discord.

Q: How do I become staff?

A: Asking to be staff isn’t the way to go about it. We pick members of the community who have shown a constant helpful attitude, and dedication to being a role model to other players. If you think you fit that bill, you can can post a staff application over at the Applications section of the forums. Just be sure to follow the Application Format.

Q: Can I roleplay on your servers?

A: Absolutely! But you must roleplay in the designated channel for said server. If the server doesn't have an RP channel, or is not designated specifically as an RP server, please refrain from RPing in the global channels. If you refuse to use the proper channels, you may be muted or tempbanned.

Q: Minecraft has updated, why hasn’t the server? When will it update?

A: The Modded Minecraft server will likely stay on 1.12.2 forever! This is just due to how mods in Minecraft work right now, and there's not really any sign of that changing.

Q: My friend/sibling/random person used my account to break your rules! Can I be unbanned?

A: Sorry, but all actions taken on your account are your responsibility, regardless of who uses it. Please refer to our full list of rules here: Square Horse Rules

Q: I need help from staff! How can I tell who is staff, and who isn’t?

A: The staff members on our Modded Minecraft server will have white letters in their title, [A] means Admin, [T] Tech, [M] Moderator, [G] means Guard, and [R] Recruit. The players with {B} in their name are not staff, but are instead some of the Builders of our server. If you need help, ask for one of the staff members with the [A], [T], [M], [G], or [R] and we’ll help you out as soon as we can- but please be patient until we can get to you. Remember, any player who does not have a [A], [M], [G], or [R] in their title is not staff- so if you see someone impersonating staff, please report them immediately.

As for Discord, staff are represented by a Red, Lime Green, Magenta, Orange, or Yellow name. You can always check if someone's staff by clicking on their name and looking at their roles. If they don't have the Staff role, they're not staff!

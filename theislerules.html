General The Isle Rules

Our general community rules are located at the top of the page!

No spawn-camping!

Avoid killing juveniles and/or hatchlings. It's hard to tell sometimes, we get that, but just be careful. If a juvenile/hatchling decides to attack you, however, it's fair game.

Using "friendly" calls doesn't prevent you from being attacked!

Lite-RP is encouraged, but not forced!

Suicide is okay!

No hacking, or abusing bugs/exploits!

Don't spam invites of any sort!

Don't attack group members unless they specify it's okay!

"Common sense" rules apply. If it's not on the list, don't automatically assume it's okay. Use your better judgment, or better yet, ask our staff!

PvP Rules

Kill on sight/killing for sport isn't tolerated. If you consistently kill other players for no reason, you will be banned. Obviously, carnivores need to eat, so "hunting" is allowed. Just kill what you need, and eat what you kill, and you'll be fine!

If you cannot completely finish eating another players' body, that's okay! Just eat what you're able! Try and get them down to the "ribs" if able, though!

Combat logging is not allowed! If you're bleeding, you're still considered in combat!

Don't pretend to be friendly with 2 calls only to attack!

Grouping Rules

Our server is very relaxed when it comes to grouping rules, as such, you may group with whatever species you'd like as any species! Herbivore and Carnivore mixed species packs/groups are a-okay here!


Misc Notes

Unlike some other servers, we're not a pay to win server! Everyone you see has gotten their dino completely legit! Donations go directly back into keeping the server up!

We're always open to suggestions on how we can improve the server! (Note, not every single request can be granted! This can be due to a multitude of reasons, but feel free to request anyway!)

False reporting is not allowed! You'll be punished if you repetitively report players for no reason!

Recording is enabled! If you report someone, please send us your recording!

These rules are subject to change, but we'll try and let you know if/when that happens!
